The early years

Alkaline trio were originally founded in late 1996 by Matt Skiba, bassist Rob Doran, and drummer Glenn Porter, in Chicago Illinois. The band released its debut single, "Sundials", in 1997. But shortly after its release, Doran departed from the band and was replaced by Dan Andriano. The band subsequently recorded an EP, For Your Lungs Only in 1998, and went on to release their debut studio album, Goddamnit later that same year. Following the release of the band's second album, Maybe I'll Catch Fire (2000), Porter left the band and was replaced by Mike Felumlee for its subsequent album, From Here to Infirmary (2001).
